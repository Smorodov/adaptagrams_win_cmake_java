\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{class_avoid_1_1_conn_end}{Conn\+End}} class represents different possible endpoints for connectors. 

Conn\+Ends may be free-\/floating points, points attached to junctions (between multiple connectors), or points attached to shapes (either to the centre of the shape or to particular pin positions on the shape).
